about me

Hi! My name is Feliclare, but you may call me Feli. I was born and raised in Waipahu, Hawai'i, located on the busy little island of O'ahu. I moved to Washington in March 2020, right before the quarantine/global shutdown due to the pandemic. Hawai'i will forever be my home, but I wanted to give myself the chance and explore the abundance of opportunities the mainland had to offer.

Fast forward to the end of 2022, and I am looking for more. I currently work for the University of Washington, Virology department as a Clinical Laboratory Technician Lead. I was interested in this coding bootcamp as a way to open new pathways for my career--maybe even change it. Let's see what the future holds for us!
